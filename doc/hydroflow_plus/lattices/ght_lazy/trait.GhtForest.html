fn find_and_force(&mut self, search_key: SearchKey) -> bool

tries to find a trie with a matching leaf. if it finds such a trie, and the search_key is longer than the height, it will force the leaf into the next trie over and recurse. returns true if it forces (1 or more times), and false otherwise.
